Crystal Collector

How to Play
  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game that you will have to match.

  • There are four crystals below. Each time a crystal is clicked, a specific amount of points will be added to your total score.

  • If you successfully match your total score to the random number, you win! If you go over the number, sorry, but you lose!

  • The value of each crystal will be hidden from you until you click on it. (Basic Math!)

  • You can play again (and again!), but be warned that the hidden points for each crystal will change each time.
